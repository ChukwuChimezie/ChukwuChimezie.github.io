Davido Announces 6th Studio Album 'ORIADÉ'

Published by Celebrity Editorial • July 2026

Afrobeats megastar Davido has officially set the global entertainment space on fire after announcing his highly anticipated 6th studio album, titled 'ORIADÉ'. The singer broke the news in an emotional viral post across his social channels, revealing the official release date of July 31, 2026.

Sponsored Content

According to close insiders from the 30BG camp, 'ORIADÉ' (which translates to "The Crowned Head" or "Royalty" in Yoruba) is poised to be Davido’s most reflective and sonically diverse body of work yet. The project reportedly bridges classic highlife melodies, modern pop-fusion accents, and energetic street-banging Amapiano rhythms.

"This album is a dedication to my journey, my family, and the incredible supporters who have worn the crown with me. We are taking the culture to a whole new dimension on July 31st." — Davido via Celebrity Insider.

Who is Featured on ORIADÉ?

While the official tracklist remains tightly under wraps, rumors have been flying wildly about several powerhouse collaborations. Highly trusted sources suggest heavy contributions from international chart-toppers as well as Nigerian hitmakers, including a rumored legendary vocal masterclass alongside YBNL lead singer Asake.

This upcoming project follows closely on the heels of the massive success of Asake & Blaqbonez's summer single "Chanel," which has dominated regional charts for three weeks straight. With July 31st locked in, the race for the undisputed sound of the year is officially heating up.
